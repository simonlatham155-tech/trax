## The Record

*What Do You See* is a collaboration between DJ Eco and Simon Latham as featured vocalist, released in 2009. It was played on A State of Trance Episode 424 — Armin van Buuren's globally syndicated trance radio show, at that point reaching tens of millions of listeners.

Simon was later included on *Armada Best Vocalist of 2012* — a compilation from the Armada Music label (Armin van Buuren's label) recognising featured vocalists across their catalogue.

## A State of Trance

A State of Trance launched in 2001 and became the central broadcast infrastructure for global trance. An ASOT play in 2009 represented a reach that had no equivalent in the 1990s progressive house circuit — worldwide simultaneous broadcast, online streaming, a documented episode archive. *New Direction* in 1998 had reached the UK via Judge Jules because the infrastructure barely existed for anything wider. By 2009 the infrastructure was global.

## The Armada Vocalist Credit

Inclusion on *Armada Best Vocalist of 2012* placed Simon alongside the vocalists associated with one of the world's most prominent trance labels. The credit documents a continued presence in the trance scene well beyond the Soundsation period — the vocal trance direction signalled by *New Direction* in 1998 had extended into a full international career as a featured vocalist and co-writer.
