## The Record

*Never Far* is a collaboration between Ari Kyle, Audioscape, and Simon Latham as featured vocalist. It was included on *In Search of Sunrise 12: Dubai* (2014).

## In Search of Sunrise

In Search of Sunrise was Tiësto's long-running DJ mix compilation series — one of the most commercially significant mix albums in trance history. Individual volumes sold hundreds of thousands of copies globally and were among the defining documents of the late 1990s and 2000s trance era.

Volume 12, set in Dubai, continued the series into 2014. Inclusion on an In Search of Sunrise compilation placed *Never Far* alongside the mainstream of international trance at that point — a different scale from the progressive house circuit of the 1990s but a direct continuation of the same genre lineage.

## The Arc

The trajectory from *New Direction* (SOS Recordings, 1998) — a full lead vocal trance record with Judge Jules plays, released before the genre had its commercial infrastructure — to *In Search of Sunrise 12* (2014) runs in a straight line. The direction was right in 1998. By 2014 the genre had built the global platform around it.
