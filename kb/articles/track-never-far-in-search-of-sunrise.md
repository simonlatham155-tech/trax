## The Record

*Never Far* is a collaboration between Ari Kyle, Audioscape, and Simon Latham as featured vocalist. It was included on *In Search of Sunrise 12: Dubai* (2014).

## In Search of Sunrise

In Search of Sunrise was Tiësto's long-running DJ mix compilation series — one of the most commercially significant mix albums in trance history. Individual volumes sold hundreds of thousands of copies globally and were among the defining documents of the late 1990s and 2000s trance era.

Volume 12, set in Dubai, continued the series into 2014. Inclusion on an In Search of Sunrise compilation placed *Never Far* alongside the mainstream of international trance at that point — a different scale from the progressive house circuit of the 1990s but a direct continuation of the same genre lineage.

## Press Review — Rachel Rixham, 8/10

> "US talent Ari Kyle returns to Magic Island after his début track Rule, this time Ari teams up with Audioscape and the popular vocalist and songwriter Simon Latham to release Never Far. The result is a high quality masterpiece that has been recently selected by Richard Durand and Lange for the highly esteemed In Search of Sunrise 12 compilation."
>
> "Never Far arrives with three remixes from German Rocking J, Koka and Mitka, an Alternative Mix and Radio Edit. Each interpretation makes the final package a great match for both progressive house and trance lovers alike. The original constructs a hands-in-the-air melody combining massive bass-lines and emotionally charged vocals. Rocking J is mind-set on creating a reaction from the club floors with his catchy-hook and amplified energy. Koka composes a minimal start that is speedily devoured by a gritty bassline and pirouetting hook. A sharp break, respectfully paves the way for Simon's vocals to sweep on through and commandeer the lead till the end. My first personal favourite is Mitka's remix, this production concentrates heavily on developing a full-on atmosphere, sedative lines meet carefully crafted percussion and intensely magnified vocals. Closing down this package comes my second personal favourite the Alternative Mix, the benevolent tones, insulated drums, and intoxicating riffs positively embrace the listener while the vocals cleanse feverishly."

The description of Simon as "the popular vocalist and songwriter" reflects the established identity by 2014 — the progression from Soundsation's unsigned white-label releases to named vocalist on a reviewed major trance compilation package.

## The Arc

The trajectory from *New Direction* (SOS Recordings, 1998) — a full lead vocal trance record with Judge Jules plays, released before the genre had its commercial infrastructure — to *In Search of Sunrise 12* (2014) runs in a straight line. The direction was right in 1998. By 2014 the genre had built the global platform around it.
