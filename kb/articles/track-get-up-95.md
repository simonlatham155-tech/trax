## Position in the Catalogue

*Get Up '95* sits between *Peace & Joy* (1994) and *Do You Feel It?* (1996) in the Soundsation catalogue. It is the first release on SOS Recordings — the independent imprint co-run by Simon Latham and Pete Lunn — before the label's third release (12SOS003) became the rare pressing that Digweed championed.

As 12SOS001 it is the foundation release of SOS Recordings, predating both the Stress Records deal and the peak of the progressive house period.
