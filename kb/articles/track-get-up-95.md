## What It Was

*Get Up '95* was the more house-leaning side of the Soundsation operation — the direction Pete Lunn and Martyn were into. SOS Recordings functioned as a proper label where different combinations of members could step in and out depending on which direction a record was going. *Get Up '95* was their territory within the label rather than the progressive house sound Simon was more centrally involved in.

Pete and Martyn later released *USA Turn It Up* and *Disco Remedy* in the same house style — SOS Recordings carrying multiple sounds under the same independent imprint.

## Position in the Catalogue

*Get Up '95* is SOS 12SOS001 — the first release on the label. The catalogue begins with the house sound before the progressive house of *Do You Feel It?* (12SOS003) arrived to define what SOS Recordings became known for externally. The label contained more than one direction from the start.
