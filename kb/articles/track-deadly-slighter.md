## The Record

*Deadly* is a collaboration between Slighter and Simon Latham. Simon has written with Slighter on multiple occasions; *Deadly* was among those collaborations — written as a record first, without a placement in mind.

The sync licensing for Lethal Weapon came later. The track was placed in Season 1 of the FOX TV series Lethal Weapon — a US network primetime drama. The placement was a result of the record existing and finding its way through sync channels, not a commission to write for the show.

## The Writing Relationship

Simon has worked with Slighter across multiple tracks, making it an ongoing collaborative relationship rather than a one-off. *Deadly* is the documented example with a confirmed major placement.

## The Sync Path

Music written independently, placed later — the standard route for sync licensing. A record made in the usual way for the usual reasons finds a secondary life when a music supervisor matches it to a scene. The FOX network placement represents mainstream US television reach — a different scale and context from the club and radio circuit, but the same music.
